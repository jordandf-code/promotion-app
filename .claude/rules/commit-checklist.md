# Commit checklist

When committing changes, update these files as applicable:

- **ALWAYS**: `docs/PLAN.md` (completion status), `CLAUDE.md` "Current status" line
- **If you added/changed a data domain or field**: `docs/DATA_MODEL.md`
- **If you added/renamed a page, route, or user-facing concept**: `docs/GLOSSARY.md`
- **If you changed AI prompts or context building**: `backend/ai/AIprompt.md`
- **If a phase/layer was completed**: `docs/PHASES_COMPLETE.md`
